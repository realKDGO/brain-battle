Why Is There a Timer?

Two or more players finished with the same score, so a tiebreaker was needed.

When is it used?
The timer only activates when players are perfectly tied on points after all words have been guessed. It is never used otherwise.

How does it work?
Each player's time is measured from the moment the game starts to the moment they correctly guess their last word. The player who completes the chain fastest wins the tiebreaker.
