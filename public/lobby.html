Dictionary Setting

Dictionary ON
The game checks if the chosen letters can make at least one real English word. This helps avoid impossible or extremely difficult letter combinations, making rounds more fair and consistent.

Keep in mind: the word list used may not include every English word, so occasionally a combination might still be rejected even if a real word exists.

Dictionary OFF
Any letter combination is allowed — even if no real English word can be formed. Players have full freedom to pick any letters they want. Some combinations may be very difficult or impossible to answer.

ℹ️ Spelling checks remain active whether Dictionary is ON or OFF. Invalid words are always rejected.
